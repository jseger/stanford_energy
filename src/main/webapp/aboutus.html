About Us

We are a team of engineers, economists, psychologists and neuroscientists at Stanford University studying how to best help people make energy-efficient purchases.

The Appliance Calculator Project is part of the Stanford Large-Scale Energy Reductions through Sensors, Feedback & Information Technology Initiative, an Advanced Research Projects Agency for Energy research program (ARPA-e), funded by the Department of Energy http://arpa-e.energy.gov/

Calculations

Energy and cost calculations are based on the best data available, but still must be considered our best estimates. We also have made a number of simplifications in the data presented to ensure that the calculator is easy to use.

Currently, computations include the average electricity required to operate the appliance over a year and the average electricity price for your state. Among others, we currently do not account for:

  1. embedded energy (energy required to build the appliance);
  2. refrigerator depreciation; and
  3. how the refrigerator is used.

Privacy

The appliances that we describe and evaluate on our website are ones offered at Sears.com. If you decide to purchase an appliance you see on our website, the transaction will be handled completely by Sears. We will not have access to any information that you provide to Sears.

No information about you as an individual or household will be recorded (i.e., your name, address etc.) from our website. However, cookies are used to provide you with a consistent experience when you make multiple visits to the website. Also, information regarding your selections on this calculator website may be recorded in order to help researchers working on this project better understand how energy information can help guide appliance purchases and future improvements to the calculator program.
